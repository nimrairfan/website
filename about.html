About Syeda Humera

Syeda Humera is a dynamic and dedicated professional with a strong commitment to empower students and society in general. She is experienced in curriculum development and implementation for grades 6 to 8 and school course programs (Concept-Based lessons and a cohesive unit of inquiry that balances curriculum demands with student agency). She has a 28 years of experience as an educationist, trainer and counselor. She presents information in a wide variety of ways, emphasizing the relevance of classroom material to the practical world. She is actively involved in designing and implementing training programs for school teachers especially for those teaching in rural areas of Sindh and Baluchistan.
